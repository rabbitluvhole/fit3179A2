Trends in Malaysia's Resale Car Market

An exploration of the resale car market in Malaysia, analyzing trends to provide insights into market dynamics.

Kuala Lumpur is the state with the highest number of cars resold per capita.

Pulau Pinang is ranked second, followed by Johor.

98% of cars resold have automatic transmission.

The highest is Honda, followed by Toyota, and Mercedes-Benz.

*Use the slider to filter car brands by the number of resales.
